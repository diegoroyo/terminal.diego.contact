<h1>Move my banana</h1>
<hr>
<p>Consider this: you manage a fruit warehouse, and need to move five boxes of oranges and three boxes of bananas from A to B. Move my banana is an app that helps you reach your fruit moving goals, activated hands-free using Natural Language Processing (NLP) algorithms.<br></p>
